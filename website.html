About RCS Solutions

Revolutionizing Business Communication

RCS (Rich Communication Services) is transforming how businesses connect with their customers. As the next-generation replacement for SMS, RCS brings rich, interactive messaging experiences directly to users' default messaging apps.

At RCS Solutions, we specialize in helping businesses leverage this powerful technology to create engaging customer experiences, drive conversions, and build stronger brand relationships through verified, interactive messaging.

Why Choose RCS?
  • Verified Business Identity: Build trust with verified sender profiles
  • Rich Media Support: Send images, videos, and interactive cards
  • Actionable Messages: Include buttons for direct user actions
  • Enhanced Analytics: Track opens, clicks, and engagement metrics
  • Wide Reach: Works on Android Messages app by default

What is RCS Messaging?

RCS (Rich Communication Services) upgrades SMS into rich, interactive & branded messaging.

What is RCS?

RCS enables images, videos, buttons, carousels and verified brand messages directly inside the default mobile Messages app.

How RCS Works
  • Create interactive campaigns
  • Send via verified RCS platform
  • Delivered through Google / Operators
  • User interacts instantly
  • Engagement analytics tracked
